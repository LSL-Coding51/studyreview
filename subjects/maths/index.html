Learn About Our Maths Course

Our maths course consists of the key concepts of maths
of each grade. Courses for Prep to Year 6, every student
will surely enjoy our maths course.

Courses for Prep

Preps will learn how to add and subtract numbers together,
as well as naming numbers during the course.

Courses for Year 1

Students in Year 1 will learn how to skip count in 2s, 5s, and
10s, starting from zero. Also learning to add simple numbers,
they will also identify Australian Coins.
